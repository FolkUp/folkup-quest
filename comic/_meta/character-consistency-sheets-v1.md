## Sin City Style Anchor

**Source:** Panel 5.1 Phase 0.5 (Library aesthetic reference)

### Core Style Requirements

**Black & White Discipline:**
- **Pure black and white only** — NO gray tones, NO halftones
- High contrast ink wash style
- Spotted blacks for large areas
- White silhouettes on black backgrounds

**Compositional Techniques:**
- **Negative space defines forms** — figures shaped by what's NOT drawn
- Heavy use of black negative space for dramatic effect
- Strong geometric compositions with dramatic perspective
- Minimal detail, maximum atmospheric impact

**Environmental Integration:**
- Characters as small silhouettes dominated by environment
- Architecture creates oppressive or dramatic scale
- Organic vs geometric contrast (like lamp in library)
- Weather effects through white splatter technique (rain, particles)

### Technical Execution Standards

**Line Quality:**
- Clean, confident ink lines
- NO sketchy or rough linework (except for atmosphere)
- Strong contrast between geometric architecture and organic elements

**Atmospheric Effects:**
- Rain: white diagonal lines/splatter technique
- Dust/particles: small white dots in black space
- Lighting: defined through pure negative space

**Panel Composition:**
- Strong perspective lines creating depth
- Characters positioned for maximum emotional impact
- Environmental elements support narrative mood
- Never crowded — embrace white/black space

## Validation Protocol

### Pre-Generation Check

Before generating any Chapter 1 panel:

1. **Character Reference:** Compare planned panel description to baseline sheets
2. **Style Anchor:** Verify Sin City technique application
3. **Consistency Scoring:** Rate expected similarity 1-10 vs canonical references
4. **Threshold Gate:** Only proceed if expected consistency ≥7/10

### Post-Generation Validation

After each panel generation:

1. **Visual Diff Check:** Side-by-side comparison with canonical panels
2. **Character Consistency:** Score 1-10 similarity to baseline sheets
3. **Style Compliance:** Verify Sin City anchor adherence
4. **Documentation:** Log consistency scores in generation manifest
5. **Threshold Decision:** ≥7/10 consistency required for approval

### Correction Protocol

If panel fails consistency threshold:

1. **Identify Drift:** Specific elements diverging from baselines
2. **Enhanced Prompts:** Add character consistency emphasis
3. **Regeneration:** Use enhanced prompt with baseline sheet references
4. **Maximum Attempts:** 2 regenerations before escalation to experts
