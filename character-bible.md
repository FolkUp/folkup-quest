## CHARACTER SPECIFICATIONS

### ALICE - Primary Protagonist

#### CORE PHYSICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- **Facial Type:** Rooney Mara inspiration - sharp, angular features
- **Face Shape:** Oval with pronounced cheekbones
- **Eyes:** Medium-set, slightly downturned, piercing expression
- **Nose:** Straight, narrow bridge
- **Mouth:** Thin lips, natural resting expression tends toward slight frown
- **Jawline:** Clean, defined but not heavy
- **Height Reference:** 5'6" equivalent in panel scale
- **Build:** Slim, precise posture, controlled movement

#### HAIR SPECIFICATIONS - LOCKED STANDARD
- **Style:** Pulled back tightly into low bun/chignon
- **Color:** Dark brown (#2C1810)
- **Texture:** Straight, sleek, no flyaways
- **Consistency Rule:** ALWAYS visible even in silhouette
- **Parting:** Center, severely straight
- **Never Varies:** Hair is NEVER loose, messy, or partially down

#### FACIAL EXPRESSION STANDARDS
- **Default Expression:** Machine-precision single line contour
- **Signature Look:** Subtle uncanny valley effect
- **Eye Expression:** Alert, calculating, slightly detached
- **Mouth Position:** Neutral to slight downward turn
- **Eyebrow Position:** Straight, minimal arch
- **Emotion Range:** Subtle variations only - Alice maintains control

#### CLOTHING STANDARDS
- **Primary Outfit:** Professional attire, muted colors
- **Color Palette:** Sage greens (#839E75), charcoal grays, deep browns
- **Signature Item:** Sage notebook/book #839E75 - ALWAYS present
- **Fit:** Tailored, never loose or casual
- **Consistency Rule:** Same outfit across all panels unless story dictates change

#### POSE VARIATIONS - RECOGNITION MAINTAINED
1. **Standing:** Straight posture, hands at sides or holding notebook
2. **Sitting:** Upright, composed, notebook accessible
3. **Walking:** Controlled stride, purposeful movement
4. **Profile:** Strong silhouette, bun clearly visible
5. **Distance Shots:** Distinctive posture and notebook visible

### АРНИ - Secondary Protagonist

#### CORE PHYSICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- **Build:** Thin, wiry physique - "beanpole" effect
- **Height Reference:** 5'10" equivalent in panel scale
- **Posture Range:** Defeated to confident variations
- **Body Language:** Expressive, reactive to circumstances
- **Facial Structure:** Angular, young adult features
- **Expression Range:** Highly variable based on emotional state

#### HAIR SPECIFICATIONS - CRITICAL CONSISTENCY FIX
- **Style:** Ponytail - MANDATORY in all panels
- **Length:** Mid-back length when loose
- **Color:** Dark brown (#3D2914)
- **Texture:** Slightly wavy, natural
- **Ponytail Position:** Mid-to-high placement on back of head
- **VISIBILITY RULE:** Ponytail MUST be visible from all angles
  - Front view: Visible over shoulder or behind head outline
  - Profile: Full ponytail silhouette clear
  - Back view: Complete ponytail visible
  - Distance: Ponytail recognizable in silhouette

#### CLOTHING STANDARDS - LOCKED SPECIFICATIONS
- **Primary Garment:** Leather jacket - CONSISTENT details required
- **Jacket Color:** Black (#1A1A1A) with subtle texture
- **Collar Style:** Standard motorcycle collar - height locked at 2.5"
- **Collar Position:** Can be up or down, but proportions consistent
- **Jacket Fit:** Slightly loose, comfortable wear
- **Texture Pattern:** Subtle leather grain, minimal shine
- **Closure:** Front zip, usually partially open
- **Under-layer:** Dark t-shirt or simple top

#### FACIAL EXPRESSION RANGE
- **Panel 1.1 Standing:** Neutral to slightly concerned
- **Panel 2.3 Defeated:** Slumped shoulders, downward gaze
- **Panel 5.1 Small Silhouette:** Recognizable posture even at distance
- **Emotional Range:** Full spectrum - reactive character
- **Consistency Factor:** Ponytail and jacket details maintain recognition

#### POSE VARIATIONS - MAINTAINING CHARACTER ID
1. **Standing Confident:** Shoulders back, hands in pockets
2. **Defeated Posture:** Slumped shoulders, head down, ponytail drooping
3. **Walking:** Casual stride, jacket movement natural
4. **Sitting:** Relaxed positioning, ponytail visible over chair/surface
5. **Distance Silhouette:** Thin build and ponytail clearly recognizable

## EMERGENCY CONSISTENCY FIXES

### If Character Recognition Fails
1. **Check silhouette first** - most critical identifier
2. **Verify signature elements** - Alice's notebook, Арни's ponytail
3. **Compare proportions** to established model
4. **Adjust expression** to match character personality
5. **Redraw if necessary** - consistency over schedule

### Common Error Prevention
- **Alice:** Never draw hair loose or messy
- **Арни:** Never hide ponytail completely
- **Both:** Maintain established clothing details
- **Proportions:** Use reference measurements consistently
