## PROMPTABILITY — REFERENCE SHEET

### Персонажи (для промптов)

**Арни:** Thin wiry man, late 30s, ponytail, short beard, leather jacket with raised collar, slouching posture, brass key on leather cord (hidden under jacket). Standard ink outline.

**Алиса:** Rooney Mara type, pulled-back hair (never loose), perfect bilateral face symmetry, unnaturally precise posture, holding brick-colored notebook with pencil. Machine-precision contour (single clean line, NOT hand-drawn feel).

**Парень с палочкой:** Soft, well-fed man ~28, slouched in chair with feet on table, smug expression, dark hoodie with startup logo, MacBook with stickers, wooden stick on table next to dirty coffee cup. Thin line weight (background character).

**Старик с замками:** Elderly figure 65+, hooded, face NEVER visible (only jaw and mouth in shadow), calloused large precise hands holding brass locks. Apron over old shirt.

**Рыбаки:** 3-5 white silhouettes on black water/horizon. No faces, no individual features. Horizontal nets.

### Стиль Sin City (для промптов)

- Pure black and white, NO gray tones, NO halftones
- High contrast ink wash style
- Negative space: figures defined by what's NOT drawn
- Spotted blacks (large areas of pure black)
- White silhouettes on black backgrounds
- Splatter technique for rain/texture (toothbrush ink)
- Double contour for weight/importance (NOT used in Chapter 1 — Арни hasn't transformed yet)
